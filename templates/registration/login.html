About aFewSteps.org:

The mission of aFewSteps.org is to raise awareness of energy use in the four communities of Nether Providence, Rose Valley, Rutledge, and Swarthmore and to provide our residents, schools, businesses, and governments with the know-how to save money and reduce greenhouse gases at least 20% by the year 2020.

About the Impact Tracker:

The impact tracker is a web application designed to make it easy to keep track of your resource usage over time. With a free account you can store information like water and electricity bills, as well as information about trash generation and heating usage. You can then compare your usage to your neighbors and the rest of the community, and learn about quick and easy ways to save resources and money.
